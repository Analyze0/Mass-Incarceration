ABOUT US
We help prevent juveniles from entering the court system by allowing parents that are in jail for non-violent charges to see and interact with their children. This is a good prevention method because children whose parents are five times more likely to get into the court system. For example Notre Dame and Americorps have partnered together to make the CHIP program that takes care of kids whose parents are incarcerated.
